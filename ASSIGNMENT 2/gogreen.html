CHALLENGE YOURSELF TO LIVE A WEEK FULL ECO-FRIENDLY. LET ’S SEE YOUR PATIENCE AND YOUR WILLPOWER TO ADOPT AN ECO-FRIENDLY - LIFE .

ENROLL YOURSELF FOR GO GREEN:





Challenge:

    Day 1: Bike Day

Challenge: Ride your cycle instead of driving for short trips.
Idea: Host a nieghbourhood bike parade or decorate your bike for added fun.

    Day 2: Plant a Tree!

Challenge: Plant a tree in your backyard or in a local green space .
Idea: Invite neighbour to join and make it a community tree-planting event.

    Day 3: Watering Day

Challenge: Water trees and plants in your neighborhood.
Idea: Use rainwater collected in buckets or set up a watering schedule with neighbors.

    Day 4: Eco-Transport Day

Challenge: Use eco-friendly transportation like walking or carpooling.
Idea: Organize a group walk or carpool for errands or outings.

    Day 5: Neighborhood Cleanup

Challenge: Pick up litter in your neighborhood or local park.
Idea: Turn it into a friendly competition or reward participants with snacks or drinks afterward.

    Day 6: Sustainable Living Tip

Challenge: Share a sustainable living tip with friends or neighbors.
Idea: Create a neighborhood chat or social media group to share tips and ideas.

    Day 7: Celebration & Reflection

Challenge: Celebrate your green efforts with a neighborhood gathering.
Idea: Host a potluck or barbecue using locally sourced or plant-based foods.
Reflect on your achievements and discuss future green goals.
